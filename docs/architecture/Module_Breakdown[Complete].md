# Section 1: No of Roles

Grad360 supports five distinct user roles. Each role is designed with clear responsibilities so governance, content operations, assessments, monitoring, and participation remain structured and accountable.

- Admin
- Contributor
- Institution
- Faculty
- Student

# Section 2: User Role and Features

## Admin
- Platform governance and role permission control.
- Institution onboarding and policy enforcement.
- Content approval, publication, and archival oversight.
- PRI and psychometric lifecycle control.
- Platform-wide analytics, compliance tracking, and escalations.

## Contributor
- Create, edit, and submit question content.
- Manage drafts and revision cycles.
- Track submission statuses and feedback.
- Improve question quality through reviewer comments.

## Institution
- Manage institutional users, batches, and academic group structure.
- Assign PRI and psychometric assessments to cohorts.
- Monitor completion and performance at institutional scope.
- Coordinate communication and escalate unresolved issues.

## Faculty
- Manage assigned cohorts and class-level readiness.
- Monitor student participation and submission behavior.
- Review outcomes and identify students requiring intervention.
- Communicate instructions and follow-ups to students.

## Student
- View and attempt assigned assessments.
- Save progress where allowed and submit final responses.
- Access published results and personal progress summaries.
- Receive notifications and raise support requests.

# Section 3: Module Breakdown

## Admin

### Module: Platform Governance
- Feature: Create, activate, suspend, and deactivate user accounts across all roles.
- Feature: Assign and revoke role permissions.
- Feature: Configure platform-level academic cycles, timelines, and policies.
- Feature: Approve and onboard institutions.

### Module: Content and Assessment Oversight
- Feature: Review, approve, reject, or request revisions for contributed questions.
- Feature: Publish and archive approved question banks.
- Feature: Control assessment availability and release schedules.
- Feature: Enforce quality standards for platform content.

### Module: PRI and Psychometric Administration
- Feature: Create and publish PRI test templates.
- Feature: Define scoring rules and result interpretation criteria.
- Feature: Manage psychometric assessment lifecycle.

### Module: Analytics and Compliance
- Feature: View platform-wide participation and performance summaries.
- Feature: Access institution-level and role-level insights.
- Feature: Monitor audit trails of key administrative actions.

### Module: Notifications and Escalations
- Feature: Publish announcements to selected roles.
- Feature: Handle escalations from institution and faculty users.

## Contributor

### Module: Question Authoring
- Feature: Create role-assigned domain questions.
- Feature: Add question context, options, and rationale.
- Feature: Save drafts before submission.

### Module: Submission Workflow
- Feature: Submit questions for admin review.
- Feature: Track question status from draft to final decision.
- Feature: Revise and resubmit based on feedback.

### Module: Work Tracking
- Feature: View personal submission history.
- Feature: Filter content by status, domain, and date.
- Feature: Reuse prior drafts for faster authoring.

### Module: Feedback Loop
- Feature: Read review comments and correction requests.
- Feature: Improve content quality through revision cycles.

## Institution

### Module: Institution Administration
- Feature: Manage institutional profile and settings.
- Feature: Create and manage faculty and student accounts.
- Feature: Organize users by program, batch, and section.

### Module: PRI Assignment Management
- Feature: Assign PRI tests to selected student groups.
- Feature: Set and monitor institutional assessment schedules.
- Feature: Track completion and pending responses.

### Module: Psychometric Assignment Management
- Feature: Assign psychometric assessments to eligible cohorts.
- Feature: Monitor assignment progress and completion status.
- Feature: Reopen eligible pending assessments when permitted.

### Module: Institutional Insights
- Feature: View institution-wide dashboards for performance and participation.
- Feature: Analyze trends by batch, faculty, and domain.
- Feature: Access report-ready summaries for academic review.

### Module: Communication and Governance
- Feature: Issue assessment instructions and deadline reminders.
- Feature: Escalate unresolved workflow issues to admin.

## Faculty

### Module: Cohort Management
- Feature: View assigned classes, sections, and student lists.
- Feature: Verify assessment readiness for assigned groups.

### Module: Assessment Monitoring
- Feature: Track student participation and completion.
- Feature: Identify non-attempted, partial, and overdue submissions.
- Feature: Send reminders within permitted workflow.

### Module: Performance Review
- Feature: View class-level PRI and psychometric outcomes.
- Feature: Compare cohort trends across assessment cycles.
- Feature: Access student-level summaries for assigned cohorts.

### Module: Academic Intervention
- Feature: Flag at-risk students based on engagement and results.
- Feature: Record intervention notes and follow-up actions.
- Feature: Share cohort observations with institution administrators.

### Module: Communication
- Feature: Share instructions and clarifications with students.
- Feature: Raise unresolved operational issues to institution users.

## Student

### Module: Assessment Dashboard
- Feature: View assigned PRI and psychometric tests.
- Feature: Track assessment status as not started, in progress, or completed.
- Feature: View active windows and deadlines.

### Module: Test Participation
- Feature: Start and complete assigned assessments.
- Feature: Save progress where policy allows.
- Feature: Submit attempts and receive submission confirmation.

### Module: Results and Progress
- Feature: View released scores and outcome summaries.
- Feature: Track personal progress across completed assessments.
- Feature: Access faculty-provided feedback where available.

### Module: Profile and Support
- Feature: Maintain personal profile details within allowed fields.
- Feature: Receive institutional announcements and reminders.
- Feature: Raise support requests for access or assessment issues.
